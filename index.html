About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Geography and Geospatial Data Science. My work lies at the intersection of climate change, carbon sink modeling, land use dynamics, and environment–human interactions.

Through advanced spatiotemporal modeling and remote sensing, I aim to unravel complex geographic phenomena and provide actionable data-driven insights for sustainable environmental management.
