About Me

Postdoctoral researcher with a Ph.D. in Geography, specializing in geospatial data science, GIS, and spatiotemporal modeling. My expertise includes:

  • Modeling and analyzing urban disasters and climate gentrification under climate change.
  • Applying machine learning and big data methods to carbon sink modeling, with strong skills in mapping and data visualization.
  • Developing comprehensive indicator systems and quantitative models to assess coastal environmental health.
  • Conducting spatiotemporal analyses of land use and land cover change and their socioeconomic impacts using satellite imagery.
