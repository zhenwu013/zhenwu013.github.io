I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Geography and Geospatial Data Science. About Me

My work lies at the intersection of climate change, carbon sink modeling, land use dynamics, urban environmental transitions, and environment–human interactions.

Through advanced spatiotemporal analysis, machine learning, and geospatial data integration, I aim to translate complex environmental patterns into rigorous, policy-relevant insights for sustainable regional development and ecosystem management.
