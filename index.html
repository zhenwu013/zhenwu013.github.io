About

Researching environmental change through geospatial intelligence

I have expertise in spatiotemporal analysis and modeling of multi-source geographic and statistical data, machine learning–based mapping of terrestrial ecosystem carbon storage, modeling and predicting climate gentrification in coastal cities, vegetation and environmental index analysis using Google Earth Engine (GEE) and open satellite data, assessing land use change driven by socioeconomic and policy factors, and evaluating the health and sustainability of coastal ecosystems.

Projects

Selected research projects

Restoration of Degraded Ecosystems and Carbon Sink Dynamics Along the Yangtze River

2022/07 – 2025/07
  • Led carbon sink modeling and estimation along the Yangtze River.
  • Analyzed spatiotemporal trends from 1990 to the present to identify degraded ecosystems and assess carbon sink dynamics.
  • Provided policy and technical recommendations to support low-carbon development strategies.

Impacts of Climate Change on Land Use in China and Carbon Neutral Response

2021/09 – 2024/06
  • Developed carbon-related models using open-source satellite data to examine land use and carbon cycle dynamics.
  • Analyzed spatiotemporal changes in land use and assessed carbon neutrality potential.
  • Coordinated research efforts and contributed to methodological design.

Spatial Data Lab Project, Harvard University

2022/09 – 2023/09
  • Led the development of Geo-Image spatial analysis tools and KNIME workflow nodes.
  • Created case studies demonstrating spatiotemporal satellite data analysis workflows.
  • Organized and delivered KNIME training workshops for researchers.
